Train Scheduler

This project involves writing a program to generate a train scheduler.

Technologies used: Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Firebase

Plan your next train voyage by choosing your train name, where you want to go, and how often the train should come. The program will then tell you when the next train should come and how long you will have to wait.
